ABOUT

Beach Hack is an annual technical hackathon organised by CODE, aimed at bringing together young minds to design innovative solutions for social problems and to help them actualise it. The first season “Beach Hack 2019” was conducted in the context of the college techfest "Techletics". The 24 hour hackathon was hosted at Munakkal Azhikode beach on 14th & 15th February 2019. The event witnessed great success and inspired us to continue this flagship project.

CODE - Community of Developers is the departmental association of Computer Science and Engineering department, Christ College of Engineering, Irinjalakuda.xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx,in accordance with this vision we have conducted various workshops, competitions, seminars and social outreach activities.

Christ College of Engineering, established in 2015, is managed by the CMI congregation of Devamatha Province, Thrissur and is part of Christ Educational and Charitable Trust, Irinjalakuda.xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx.

Rules and Regulations

1.Beach hack challenge is open to all students currently enrolled in any undergraduate programme.
2.The application of a team must be submitted on the portal by 6/2/2020.
3.Each team should have a leader and a team must comprise a minimum of 4 members and a maximum of 6 members.
4.Maximum of 20 teams will be selected from the applications on the basis of the attached resume. Selected teams will be notified by 8/2/2020.
5.Selected teams should pay the registration fees of Rs.750 (or as applicable) by 10/2/2020, failure of which will result in the cancellation of their application.
6.Refund of the registration fee will not be issued.
7.The team leader should inform about any changes to the team members to the concerned authorities by 13/2/2020.
8.College ID is mandatory for all team members.
9.At the beginning of the hackathon, the teams will be given a list of problem statements related to the theme, out of which they have to choose one.
10.Once a problem statement is selected by a team, it cannot be changed.
11.There will be 2 rounds of review and one round of judgement.
12.During the review rounds the teams will be guided and their progress will be evaluated.
13.The final judgement will be based on: aptness, novelty & feasibility.
14.Decisions made by Judges will be final.
